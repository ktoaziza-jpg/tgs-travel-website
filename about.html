About TGS Travel

TGS Travel agency has passed a long way since its early beginnings in 2010. The company was founded by people passionate about tourism and lifetime adventures.

At the moment, the company serves more than 6,000 travelers across Europe and Asia each year. We serve clients worldwide.

Our professional team has been lucky to travel to a number of amazing countries across all continents and can proudly boast many years of practical expertise in the travel industry. They are committed to excellence in providing clients with high-quality travel and the best possible traveling experience at reasonable prices. Due to the time spent abroad, they clearly understand all the demands of a modern traveler. Our specialists are always friendly and pay individual attention to each client. Expert guides will happily share with you all of their favourite spots.

Georgia

Where Majestic Peaks Meet the Birthplace of Wine.

A Tapestry of Culture and Nature

Georgia is a country of staggering beauty and profound contrasts. Nestled between the soaring peaks of the Caucasus Mountains and the shores of the Black Sea, it offers an adventure for every type of traveler.

Wander through the cobbled streets and pastel-colored balconies of Old Tbilisi, hike up to the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church standing sentinel above the clouds, or explore the ancient cave cities carved deep into rock.

Known as the cradle of wine, Georgia invites you to partake in a traditional Supra feast, where polyphonic singing and endless toasts celebrate the joy of life. Discover true Georgian hospitality with TGS Travel.
